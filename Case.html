数据:

  1. When the light ray illustrated in figure passes through the glass block of index of refraction $$n = 1.50$$, it is shifted laterally by the distance $$d$$. Find the time interval required for the light to pass through the glass block.
  2. A: "$$1.33\times 10^{-10} \text{ s}$$",
  3. B: "$$2.06\times 10^{-10} \text{ s}$$",
  4. C: "$$1.06\times 10^{-10} \text{ s}$$",
  5. D: "$$1.92\times 10^{-10} \text{ s}$$"
  6. Please answer the question directly with only the letter of the correct option and nothing else.

多模态输入:

COT:

Model_answer:

  1. To solve this problem, we need to determine the time interval required for the light to pass through the glass block. Here's the step-by-step reasoning:
  2. Identify the given parameters:
    • Index of refraction of the glass, $$n = 1.50$$
    • Thickness of the glass block, $$t = 2.00 \,\text{cm}=0.02\,\text{m}$$
    • Angle of incidence, $$\theta_i = 30.0^\circ$$
  3. Use Snell's Law to find the angle of refraction:
    • $$n_1\sin\theta_i=n_2\sin\theta_r$$ where $$n_1 = 1$$ (air) and $$n_2 = 1.50$$ (glass).
    • $$1 \cdot \sin 30.0^\circ = 1.50 \cdot \sin \theta_r$$
    • $$\sin \theta_r = \frac{\sin 30.0^\circ}{1.50} = \frac{0.5}{1.50} = \frac{1}{3}$$
    • $$\theta_r = \sin^{-1}\left(\frac{1}{3}\right) \approx 19.47^\circ$$
  4. Calculate the path length inside the glass block:
    • The light travels along a slanted path inside the glass block. The path length $$L$$ can be found using the thickness $$t$$ and the angle of refraction $$\theta_r$$:
    • $$L = \frac{t}{\cos \theta_r}$$
    • $$\cos \theta_r = \cos 19.47^\circ \approx 0.943$$
    • $$L = \frac{0.02 \, \text{m}}{0.943} \approx 0.0212 \, \text{m}$$
  5. Calculate the speed of light in the glass:
    • The speed of light in the glass $$v$$ is given by: $$v = \frac{c}{n}$$
    • where $$c = 3 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s}$$ is the speed of light in vacuum.
    • $$v = \frac{3 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s}}{1.50} = 2 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s}$$
  6. Calculate the time interval required for the light to pass through the glass block:
    • The time $$t$$ is given by: $$t = \frac{L}{v}$$
    • $$t = \frac{0.0212 \, \text{m}}{2 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s}} = 1.06 \times 10^{-10} \, \text{s}$$
  7. Thus, the correct option is \boxed{C}.
